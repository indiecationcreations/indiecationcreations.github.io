Factsheet: Legend vs. Legion

  • Developer & Publisher: Indiecation Creations
  • Demo Release: Q1 2025
  • Full Release: Q2 2025
  • Platforms: PC (Steam page)
  • Genre: Tower Defense, Roguelite, Arcade

Description

The realm of Valia has been ravaged by a horde of monsters, ghouls, and vegetables(!) under the control of an nameless evil. Nobody can stop it... except for you.

Legend vs. Legion is a roguelite tower defense... without towers. You'll take direct control of a legendary hero, choosing from an arsenal of randomly selected spells and artifacts to slay invaders.

Each run presents new challenges and opportunities; you'll have to carefully choose your spells and artifacts to shape your strategy. Will you decimate your enemies with arcane magic, crush them with the might of your blade?

Key Features:

  • A roguelite twist: each playthrough offers a unique challenge with randomly chosen abilities + artifacts.
  • Easy to pick up, hard to master: you'll need strategy, reflexes, and resilience to survive against the Legion.
  • High replayability, countless synergies: over 160+ enemies to encounter, and dozens of spells and artifacts that synergize in unique ways.
  • Character progression: unlock and upgrade your arsenal to become the ultimate defender of the realm.
