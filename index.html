  • Demo Release: Q1 2026
  • Full Release: Q2 2026

  • Genre: Tower Defense, Roguelite, Arcade

Description

The realm of Valia is under siege! Flame-spewing peppers, land-walking fishfolk, and endlessly-reanimating skeletons are just a few of the bizarre creatures swarming the land. Only you can vanquish the horde and restore peace to the world.

Legend vs. Legion is a roguelite tower defense without towers. You'll take direct control of a legendary hero, choosing from an arsenal of randomly selected spells and artifacts to slay invaders.

Each run presents new challenges and opportunities; you'll have to carefully choose your spells and artifacts to shape your strategy. Will you decimate your enemies with arcane magic, or crush them with the might of your blade?

Key Features:

  • A roguelite twist: each playthrough offers a unique challenge with randomly chosen abilities + artifacts.
  • Easy to pick up, hard to master: you'll need strategy, reflexes, and resilience to survive against the Legion.
  • High replayability, countless synergies: over 160+ enemies to encounter, and dozens of spells and artifacts that synergize in unique ways.
  • Character progression: unlock and upgrade your arsenal by completing side missions, to become the most powerful defender of the realm.
