Description

"Legend vs. Legion" is a hybrid tower defense game inspired by classics like *Warcraft 3* hero defense mods, *Plants vs. Zombies*, and *Space Invaders*. In this game, players control a Hero who casts spells, acquires powerful artifacts, and engages in Soulslike combat to defend their base against waves of invading forces. Test your strategy, reflexes, and adaptability as you battle through relentless enemies and earn your place as a true Legend.
